# Best No-Code Chatbot Builders in 2025: Build AI Chatbots Without Coding

Chatbots handle customer inquiries 24/7, qualify leads, and book meetings — all without human intervention. The best no-code chatbot builders let you deploy AI-powered bots in hours, not weeks. Monthly search volume: ~25,000/month.

## What Can No-Code Chatbots Do?

- Answer FAQs with knowledge base integration
- Qualify leads and route to sales reps
- Book meetings via calendar integration
- Process orders and handle returns
- Provide personalized product recommendations
- Collect feedback and survey responses

---

## Top 7 No-Code Chatbot Builders for 2025

### 1. Tidio — Best for Small Business Customer Support

**Best for:** E-commerce and small businesses wanting live chat + AI chatbot combined

Tidio combines live chat, AI chatbot (Lyro), and email marketing in one platform. Its Lyro AI can handle up to 70% of customer inquiries automatically using your existing support documentation.

**Strengths:**
- Lyro AI handles common questions automatically
- Live chat and chatbot in one platform
- E-commerce integrations (Shopify, WooCommerce)
- Visual flow builder for custom conversations
- Free plan with generous limits

**Limitations:**
- Lyro AI conversations limited on lower plans
- Advanced automation requires higher tiers
- Design customization limited

**Pricing:** Free (50 conversations/month); Starter $29/month; Growth $59/month

---

### 2. ManyChat — Best for Social Media Chatbots

**Best for:** Businesses running Instagram DM, Facebook Messenger, and WhatsApp automation

ManyChat dominates social media chatbot automation. Its visual flow builder creates automated conversations across Instagram, Facebook Messenger, WhatsApp, and SMS — capturing leads and nurturing customers where they already spend time.

**Strengths:**
- Best-in-class Instagram DM automation
- Facebook Messenger flows
- WhatsApp Business integration
- Keyword triggers and comment automation
- Built-in broadcast messaging (email + SMS + messenger)

**Limitations:**
- Focused on social media — limited for website chat
- Pricing scales with subscriber count
- Some features platform-dependent (Meta API changes)

**Pricing:** Free (1,000 contacts); Pro from $15/month (scales with contacts)

---

### 3. Chatbase — Best for AI Chatbots Trained on Your Content

**Best for:** SaaS companies and content-heavy businesses wanting a ChatGPT-powered bot from their docs

Chatbase lets you create a custom ChatGPT chatbot trained on your website, PDFs, and documentation. Upload your content, get a chatbot that accurately answers questions about your product — no conversation flow design needed.

**Strengths:**
- Train chatbot on your own documents and URLs
- GPT-4 powered responses
- Embed on any website
- Sources cited in responses
- Lead capture integration

**Limitations:**
- Less control over conversation flow vs. Landbot
- Requires good source documentation
- Can hallucinate on edge cases

**Pricing:** Free (20 messages/month); Hobby $19/month; Standard $49/month

---

### 4. Landbot — Best for Conversational Forms and Lead Gen

**Best for:** Marketing teams wanting beautiful conversational forms that feel like real chat

Landbot turns standard forms into engaging conversational experiences. Instead of a boring contact form, visitors get a step-by-step chat interface that dramatically improves completion rates.

**Strengths:**
- Beautiful conversational form experience
- Visual drag-and-drop flow builder
- WhatsApp and website deployment
- Logic branching and calculations
- HubSpot, Salesforce, Zapier integrations

**Limitations:**
- Not a traditional AI chatbot (more scripted flows)
- Expensive for small teams
- WhatsApp feature requires Meta approval

**Pricing:** Sandbox (free, 100 chats/month); Starter $42/month; Pro $105/month

---

### 5. Intercom — Best for B2B Customer Support at Scale

**Best for:** B2B SaaS companies wanting AI-first customer support with CRM integration

Intercom's Fin AI Agent handles support inquiries using your help center content, escalating to human agents only for complex issues. Its inbox, knowledge base, and analytics are best-in-class for growing SaaS.

**Strengths:**
- Fin AI Agent resolves 50%+ of inquiries automatically
- Unified inbox for all channels
- Proactive messaging and product tours
- CRM and Salesforce integration
- Customer data platform built-in

**Limitations:**
- Expensive — starts at $74/month + usage fees
- Complex for simple use cases
- Fin AI billed per resolution (adds up)

**Pricing:** Essential $74/month; Advanced $169/month; Expert $359/month + Fin AI $0.99/resolution

---

### 6. Botpress — Best Open-Source Chatbot Builder

**Best for:** Technical founders and developers who want maximum control over a free platform

Botpress is open-source and free to host yourself, with a cloud version offering a generous free tier. Its GPT-Native engine builds AI chatbots that understand context and handle complex conversations.

**Strengths:**
- Open-source — free to self-host
- GPT-Native AI with natural language understanding
- Visual flow builder + code when needed
- Integrates with WhatsApp, Slack, Teams, web
- Strong developer community

**Limitations:**
- More technical than Tidio or ManyChat
- Self-hosting requires server management
- Enterprise features paid

**Pricing:** Free cloud tier (5 bots, 2K messages/month); Teams $495/month; Enterprise custom

---

### 7. Voiceflow — Best for Multi-Channel AI Agents

**Best for:** Product teams building sophisticated AI agents across voice, chat, and SMS

Voiceflow is a professional platform for designing and deploying AI agents. Used by companies like JP Morgan and DoorDash, it handles complex multi-turn conversations across web, mobile, Alexa, and phone.

**Strengths:**
- Professional-grade AI agent design
- Multi-channel: web, voice, phone, SMS
- Knowledge base integration
- Team collaboration features
- API and webhook integrations

**Limitations:**
- Complex for simple use cases
- Requires investment in learning the platform
- Expensive for small teams

**Pricing:** Sandbox (free, 2 editors); Pro $50/editor/month; Business custom

## FAQ

### What is the best free no-code chatbot builder?

Tidio offers the most value on a free plan for website chat. ManyChat's free plan is excellent for social media. Botpress is the best free option if you're comfortable with self-hosting.

### Can no-code chatbots use AI?

Yes — most modern no-code chatbot builders are AI-powered. Chatbase uses GPT-4, Tidio has Lyro AI, Intercom has Fin AI, and Botpress uses a GPT-Native engine. The era of rigid decision-tree bots is largely over.

### How much does it cost to build a chatbot without code?

Simple chatbots: $0-$30/month using Tidio, ManyChat, or Chatbase free plans. Business-grade chatbots: $50-$200/month. Enterprise: $500+/month. You can launch something functional for free in most cases.
